Corporate sustainability reporting largely focuses on how business operations impact the environment. As the effects of climate change become more apparent, greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions data helps translate business activities into climate impact. Regulators are likely to implement more rules around mandatory disclosure of emissions data that quantifies the climate impacts of business operations
